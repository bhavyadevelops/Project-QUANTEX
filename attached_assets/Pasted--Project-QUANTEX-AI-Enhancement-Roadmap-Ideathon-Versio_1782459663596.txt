# Project QUANTEX – AI Enhancement Roadmap (Ideathon Version)

## Objective

Transform Project QUANTEX from a technician booking platform into an AI-powered intelligent technical support ecosystem.

The current MVP successfully demonstrates technician discovery and service booking. This enhancement phase focuses on integrating AI capabilities that significantly improve user experience, technician efficiency, and platform intelligence while maximizing innovation for ideathon evaluation.

---

# Feature Priority Order

Only implement features listed under Phase 1 during this development cycle.

---

# Phase 1 – High Priority Features

## 1. AI Issue Diagnosis (Highest Priority)

### Goal

Allow users to instantly understand their technical issue before booking a technician.

### Inputs

* Upload Image
* Voice Description
* Text Description

### AI Should Generate

* Probable issue
* Confidence score
* Severity Level
* Estimated repair cost
* Estimated repair duration
* Suggested temporary safety precautions
* Recommendation whether technician visit is required

### UI Requirements

* Clean AI diagnosis card
* Confidence indicator
* Severity badge
* Cost estimation
* Duration estimation
* Recommended next action

---

## 2. Intelligent Technician Matching

### Goal

Automatically recommend the most suitable technician instead of simply showing the nearest one.

### Matching Factors

* Specialization
* Experience
* Rating
* Success Rate
* Response Time
* Availability
* Distance
* Estimated Arrival Time
* Price Range

### UI Requirements

Display "AI Recommended Technician" at the top with an explanation of why they were selected.

---

## 3. Emergency Detection System

### Goal

Automatically detect dangerous situations and prioritize emergency requests.

### Emergency Categories

* Electrical Sparks
* Gas Leakage
* Fire Hazard
* Water Pipe Burst
* Short Circuit
* Smoke Detection

### Features

* Emergency badge
* Queue prioritization
* Safety instructions
* Fast technician dispatch
* Emergency contact suggestions if applicable

---

## 4. AI Technical Assistant

### Goal

Reduce unnecessary technician visits through intelligent troubleshooting.

### Capabilities

* Interactive conversation
* Ask diagnostic questions
* Recommend simple troubleshooting
* Request additional images
* Escalate to technician booking when required

### User Flow

Describe Problem

↓

AI asks follow-up questions

↓

Problem resolved OR technician booking

---

## 5. Technician AI Assistant

### Goal

Improve technician productivity before arrival.

### Technician Dashboard

Display

* Customer issue summary
* Previous service history
* AI diagnosis
* Estimated repair duration
* Suggested spare parts
* Safety recommendations

---

## 6. Analytics Dashboard

### Goal

Provide platform-wide insights for administrators.

### Metrics

* Total Requests
* Completed Jobs
* Pending Jobs
* Emergency Requests
* Average Response Time
* Technician Performance
* Customer Satisfaction
* AI Diagnosis Accuracy
* Average Repair Cost
* Most Common Issues

Use charts and KPI cards with responsive design.

# Success Criteria

The platform should clearly demonstrate that QUANTEX is no longer just a service marketplace but an AI-powered technical assistance ecosystem.

The final experience should communicate three core messages:

1. AI understands technical problems before humans intervene.
2. AI intelligently connects users with the best technician.
3. AI improves the efficiency of every stakeholder, including customers, technicians, and administrators.

Every new feature should reinforce these principles while maintaining a clean, intuitive, and professional user experience suitable for an innovation-focused ideathon.
