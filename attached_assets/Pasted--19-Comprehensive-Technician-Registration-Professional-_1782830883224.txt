# 19. Comprehensive Technician Registration & Professional Profile

The current technician registration form is too limited. Redesign it into a complete professional onboarding experience while keeping it simple, intuitive, and mobile-friendly.

The goal is to collect enough information to build a trustworthy technician profile and improve AI-powered matching, pricing, and customer confidence.

## Personal Information

* Full Name
* Profile Photo
* Date of Birth
* Gender (Optional)
* Mobile Number
* Email Address
* Government ID Verification
* Address
* Current City
* PIN Code

---

## Professional Information

Allow technicians to clearly define what they do.

Examples:

* Electrician
* Plumber
* Carpenter
* AC Technician
* Appliance Repair
* Computer Repair
* Laptop Repair
* Mobile Repair
* CCTV Installation
* RO Service
* Painter
* Welder
* Mason
* Interior Work
* Furniture Assembly
* Mechanic
* Solar Technician
* Internet & Networking
* Smart Home Installation
* Pest Control
* Cleaning Services
* Gardening
* Home Automation
* Custom Service (Other)

Support multiple professions if applicable.

---

## Services Offered

Each technician should be able to select multiple services under their profession.

Example:

Electrician

* Fan Installation
* Switch Repair
* Wiring
* MCB Replacement
* Meter Installation
* Short Circuit Repair
* Emergency Electrical Service

Computer Repair

* Virus Removal
* SSD Upgrade
* RAM Upgrade
* Windows Installation
* Data Recovery
* Laptop Screen Replacement
* Motherboard Repair

The list of services should dynamically adapt based on the selected profession, with an option to add custom services.

## Experience

Collect:

* Years of Experience
* Certifications
* Training
* Company Previously Worked For (Optional)
* Areas of Expertise
* Languages Spoken

---

## Pricing

Allow technicians to define how they charge.

Supported pricing models:

* Fixed Visit Charge
* Hourly Rate
* Per Job
* Inspection Charge
* Emergency Service Charge
* Weekend/Holiday Charge
* Night Service Charge

Example:

Visit Charge:
₹200

Hourly:
₹500/hour

Emergency:
₹100 extra

The platform should display these charges transparently before booking.

Technicians should configure:

* Working Days

* Emergency Availability
* Vacation Mode
* Maximum Daily Bookings
* Maximum Travel Distance (Service Radius)

Example:

Monday–Saturday

9:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Emergency:
Yes

Maximum Radius:
20 km

## Service Area

Allow technicians to specify:

* Current Location
* Preferred Service Locations
* Maximum Travel Radius
* Multiple Cities (if applicable)

Display this information during customer searches.

## Equipment & Capabilities

Technicians can indicate:

* Own Vehicle
* Bike
* Car
* Van
* Tools Available
* Specialized Equipment
* Heavy Machinery

## Portfolio

Allow technicians to upload:

* Previous Work Photos
* Certifications
* Licenses
* Awards
* Project Images
* Before & After Images

Display these in their public profile.

---

## Identity & Trust

Support verification for:

* Government ID
* Phone Number
* Email
* Address
* Professional Certificates
* Business Registration (Optional)

Verified technicians should receive trust badges.

---

## Banking & Payments

Securely collect:

* UPI ID
* Bank Account Details (if required)
* Preferred Payment Methods
* GST Number (Optional)

Ensure sensitive information is encrypted and not publicly visible.

---

## Professional Profile Preview

Before submitting, show technicians a preview of their public profile exactly as customers will see it.

Include:

* Name
* Photo
* Profession
* Skills
* Services
* Pricing
* Experience
* Certifications
* Availability
* Ratings (once available)
* Portfolio
* Verification Badges

Allow edits before final submission.

---

## Editable Profile

After registration, technicians should be able to update:

* Services
* Prices
* Availability
* Portfolio
* Certifications
* Service Radius
* Working Hours
* Contact Preferences
* Emergency Availability

Changes should sync instantly across the platform.

---

## AI Integration

The AI assistant should use technician profile data to improve recommendations by matching:

* Required service
* Relevant skills
* Experience
* Customer location
* Service radius
* Pricing
* Availability
* Ratings
* Estimated arrival time

This enables intelligent technician recommendations rather than simply listing every available technician.
